CHHAAVA

Film Overview

CHHAAVA is a 2025 Indian Hindi-language film on the life of Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj. It is directed by Laxman Utekar, Vicky Kaushal played the role of Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj It was released in theatres on 14 February 2025.

"The film is based on the real life of Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj, the son of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj."

Chhaava has been a super box office success, earning over ₹600 crore in India and exceeding ₹800 crore worldwide. It has been described as one of the biggest hits of 2025.
